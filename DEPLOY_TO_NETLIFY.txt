DEPLOY QC TIME TRACKER TO NETLIFY DROP
=======================================

This is the FASTEST way - just drag and drop!
Takes less than 2 minutes and it's FREE forever.

STEP 1: Go to Netlify Drop
---------------------------
1. On your laptop, open browser and go to:
   https://app.netlify.com/drop

2. No account needed to start!

STEP 2: Prepare Your Files
---------------------------
1. Open File Explorer
2. Go to: C:\Users\nevho\code\Time_Tracker
3. Select these 5 files (hold Ctrl and click each):
   ✓ time_tracker_pwa.html
   ✓ manifest.json
   ✓ service-worker.js
   ✓ QC_Logo_Letterhead_241111.jpg
   ✓ QC_Letters_250212.ico

STEP 3: Deploy
--------------
1. Drag the 5 selected files onto the Netlify Drop page
2. Wait 10-30 seconds for upload
3. Netlify will give you a URL like:
   https://random-name-12345.netlify.app

4. Copy this URL!

STEP 4: Rename Your Site (Optional)
------------------------------------
1. Click "Sign up" or "Log in" on Netlify (free account)
2. Go to "Site settings" → "Change site name"
3. Change to something like: qc-time-tracker
4. Your new URL: https://qc-time-tracker.netlify.app

STEP 5: Install on Android
---------------------------
1. Open Chrome on your Android phone
2. Go to your Netlify URL
3. Add /time_tracker_pwa.html at the end
   Example: https://qc-time-tracker.netlify.app/time_tracker_pwa.html
4. Tap menu (⋮) → "Install app"
5. Done!

BENEFITS:
---------
✓ Easiest method - just drag & drop
✓ No technical knowledge needed
✓ Free forever
✓ HTTPS enabled automatically
✓ Works immediately
✓ Can update by dragging new files

UPDATING THE APP:
-----------------
1. Go to your Netlify site dashboard
2. Click "Deploys" tab
3. Drag your updated files to the drop zone
4. Done! Changes are live in seconds
